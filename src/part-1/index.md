This unit introduces the fundamental concepts of microeconomic analysis: how markets work, how prices are determined, and how consumers and firms respond to incentives.

## Topics Covered

- Supply and demand analysis
- Price elasticity
- Consumer theory and utility maximization
- Production and cost theory
