This unit applies microeconomic tools to evaluate market outcomes and government interventions. We ask: when do markets fail to maximize social welfare, and what policy instruments are available to address these failures?

## Topics Covered

- Welfare analysis: consumer and producer surplus
- Externalities and Pigouvian taxes
- Public goods and the free-rider problem
- Information asymmetries
