This unit examines how firm behavior and market outcomes differ depending on the degree of competition. We move from the ideal benchmark of perfect competition to the polar case of monopoly and the continuum in between.

## Topics Covered

- Perfect competition and efficiency
- Monopoly and deadweight loss
- Oligopoly and strategic interaction
- Monopolistic competition
